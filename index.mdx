AI Job Search is an open-source framework that turns [Claude Code](https://claude.com/claude-code) into a full-stack job application assistant. Fork the repo, run `/setup` to build your profile, and let Claude handle the heavy lifting — from scraping job boards to drafting and compiling tailored PDFs.

## The Core Workflow

Three commands cover the full job application lifecycle from search to submission.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Build your profile with /setup">
    Run `/setup` inside Claude Code to populate your candidate profile. Choose from three onboarding paths: read your `documents/` folder, import a single CV, or walk through a structured interview. Your profile drives every downstream command.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find matching jobs with /scrape">
    Run `/scrape` to search multiple job portals simultaneously. Claude deduplicates results and presents them sorted by fit. Use `/rank` to batch-score all results before deciding where to apply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply with /apply">
    Pass a job URL or paste a posting to `/apply`. Claude evaluates your fit, drafts a tailored CV and cover letter in LaTeX, spawns a reviewer agent to critique the drafts, revises based on feedback, compiles both PDFs, and runs an ATS parseability check — all automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Makes This Different

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Drafter-Reviewer Pipeline" icon="users">
    Two separate Claude agents collaborate on every application: one drafts, one researches the company and critiques. The drafter then revises. Single-pass generation never reaches this quality.
  </Card>
  <Card title="PDF Verification Loop" icon="file-check">
    Every application compiles and visually inspects the PDFs. Orphaned job titles, overflowing cover letters, and font mismatches are fixed automatically before you ever see the output.
  </Card>
  <Card title="ATS Text-Layer Check" icon="magnifying-glass">
    `/apply` extracts the compiled CV's text layer with `pdftotext` and verifies that contact details, reading order, and posting keywords survive the way an ATS parser sees them.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extensible by Design" icon="plug">
    Add your own job portals with `/add-portal`, swap in your own LaTeX templates with `/add-template`, and expand your profile with `/expand`. The framework grows with your needs.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Extended Commands

Once your profile is in place, six more commands extend the framework:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="/outcome" icon="chart-line" href="/commands/outcome">
    Log interview stages, offers, and rejections. Archives submitted materials and calibrates your fit framework over time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="/expand" icon="magnifying-glass-plus" href="/commands/expand">
    Enriches your profile by scanning GitHub, LinkedIn, and other public sources you've linked.
  </Card>
  <Card title="/upskill" icon="graduation-cap" href="/commands/upskill">
    Analyzes skill gaps between your profile and target postings, with a prioritized learning plan.
  </Card>
  <Card title="/add-portal" icon="globe" href="/commands/add-portal">
    Scaffolds a job-portal search skill for any job board in any country.
  </Card>
  <Card title="/add-template" icon="file-code" href="/commands/add-template">
    Register your own LaTeX CV or cover letter template and wire it into `/apply`.
  </Card>
  <Card title="/reset" icon="rotate-left" href="/commands/reset">
    Wipe profile data, documents, or both — with a mandatory confirmation step.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
